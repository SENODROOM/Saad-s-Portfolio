Welcome TO
The Trust School

Our mission is to provide quality education free of cost or at subsidized rates on no profit basis to the students from the underprivileged section of the society.

Visions

TEDDS first and foremost responsibility is to the deprived children of Pakistan who will be fostered and nurtured in our schools. They must be given complete, all-embracing and comprehensive education.
The best skills on modern and academic subjects to prepare for the higher endeavors in life, also the more traditional knowledge of language, literature, art and humanities to further their personalities in taking up social responsibilities of the future. This must be done in a disciplined, harmonious and a clean environment free from disturbances and distractions.
The second responsibility is to our donors, members, advisors and participants. The resources of TEDDS shall only be employed EXCLUSIVELY to create opportunities and advancement for children from the under-privileged segment of the Pakistani society.
The funds employed shall be fully optimized – austerity in expenses, without compromising basic needs and essentials in achieving quality results. The procedures adopted shall always be absolutely transparent and compliant to the objectives of the Trust. Financial discipline and sincerity to the purpose is our promise.
Our responsibilities extend to our teachers and faculty members who are so very critical in achieving our mission. The faculty of TEDDS must be experienced and knowledgeable to meet the standards of their discipline while compassionate and kind to fulfill the role of a teacher, guide and an educator.
They must be appropriately compensated and continuously trained to improve upon their own knowledge and skill sets.
TEDDS’ responsibilities are to all other agencies, private or government; to fulfill all our obligations- legal or financial. To be positive and constructive members to the communities and the cities that house our schools.
And finally our vouch is to Pakistan – in providing an avenue of future and progress for its people.

Missions

The mission of TEDDS is to provide quality education at subsidized rates, free of cost or concessional rates on no profit basis, to the meritorious students from the underprivileged section of the society.
Academic excellence, wholesome growth of personality, leadership development and character building enabling high standing in the future professional career of the budding students, is the prime target of The Trust School System.
Purpose of Establishment
TEDDS has been founded principally
  1. To provide quality education to talented children of the under-privileged and deserving lot of our society.
  2. To promote educational interest and to enhance the standard of education and status of teachers in the society.
  3. To coordinate with like-minded individuals / organizations for establishment of educational institutions in their respective areas to achieve and supplement the noble mission of TEDDS.

Our Collaborations

Nestlé for Healthier Kids
LUMS Corner
The Trust School is proud to share its continued partnership with Nestlé for its program “Nestlé for Healthier Kids (N4HK)”, which empowers parents, teachers and caregivers to foster healthier eating, drinking and lifestyle habits among children. The Trust School is continuously strengthening its efforts to provide knowledge on healthy nutrition and its benefits, to children through fun filled activities and vocational training.
