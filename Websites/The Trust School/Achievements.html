Academics and Distinguished Achievements

The Trust School recognizes potential in every student and encourages them to pursue their personal best at every opportunity.

Academics and Scolastic

  • Since TEDDS inception, over 3,464 students (upto 2020) have appeared in the Matriculation(SSC) exams with 100% pass results in BISE Lahore.
  • Admission of pass out students in engineering, medical and other professional institutions LUMS, KE, FAST, UET, GIK, NUST, and other on merit.
  • Associated with the “Seeds of Peace International” programs since 2002. Attended camps in USA and India.
  • Beneficiary of National Outreach program of LUMS and Chandbagh School.
  • Several awards at National Museum of Science and Technology, Lahore.
  • Presidential Gold Medal in Scouting.
  • Numerous awards in Speech, Quiz, Qirat, Naat and Poetry competitions.
  • Initiated multiple inter- school competitions such as the “Brain of Lahore”, “Orator of Lahore” etc. to encourage intellectual, social and moral development of children.

Publications and Creative Work

  • Hayyat-e-javed, Biography of Sir syed Ahmed Khan(reprinted)
  • Azmat – e- Insan, and ‘Science Kahani’ teaching programs developed by TEDDS.
  • Dictionary of English language creative work.
  • TEDDS project used as a reference subject for M. Ed. Research thesis papers.
  • A book on “Some Common Expression of English Language”

Other Distinguished Achievements

  • TEDDS Ex-student Topped NTS Medical Entry Test 2007 by scoring highest ever marks.
  • Admission in the top Professional Institutions like LUMS, FAST, KEMC & UET etc.
  • Lahore University of Management Sciences (LUMS) has so far selected 36 students from TEDDS under its National Outreach Program (NOP).
    • Four students graduated from LUMS have earned lucrative professional appointments in Middle East and Canada.
  • The Trust School student Muhammad Abdur Rauf hired by Microsoft as a Software Development Engineer.
  • Consistent Top Positions in All Pakistan Science Model & Quiz Competitions by National Museum of Science and Technology, Lahore Pakistan.
  • Presidential Gold Medal in Scouting & representation in South Korea Jamboree.
  • Attended a number of foreign camps under “Seeds of Peace International”.
  • TEDDS introduced innovative Championship Programs viz “Brain of Lahore”, “Orator of Lahore”, “Extempore Essay Writing Contest” .
  • Coveted International Scholarships like “FULL BRIGHT” won by TEDDS EX-Student.
